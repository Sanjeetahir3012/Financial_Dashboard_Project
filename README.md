## **Objective**

- To provide a clear and interactive view of organizational financial performance, helping stakeholders identify budget deviations, overspending, savings, and overall    departmental performance for better decision-making.

## **Key Insights**

- Total Budget Amount: 548M
- Total Actual Amount: 558M
- Total Variance: 9M
- Average Budget Utilization: 101.68%

## **Dashboard Features**

- Department-wise Budget vs Actual comparison
- Variance & Variance% KPIs for performance tracking
- Savings by Department pie chart
- Trend analysis using line chart
- Monthly slicer for interactive filtering
- Clean KPIs panel for quick overview

## **Tools & Technologies**

- Power BI Desktop
- Data Modeling & Data Cleaning
- DAX Measures (Saving, Average Budget Utilization%)
- Visualizations: Pie Chart, Line Chart, KPI Cards, Slicers
